POLAR BEAR

Polar bears have black skin and although their fur appears white, it is actually transparent.It is the largest carnivore on land.

  • Scientific name: Ursus maritimus
  • Average Length:2 to 3 m
  • Average lifespan: 15 to 18 years
  • Habitat: the Arctic

The polar bear largely lies within the Arctic Circle.A boar (adult male) weighs around 350 to 700 kg ,while a sow (adult female) is about half that size. Although it is the sister species of the brown bear,it has evolved to occupy a narrower ecological niche, with many body characteristics adapted for cold temperatures and for hunting seals.
